A quick run down

Aradiabot is a bot designed for speed, reliablility and fun. In the last 2 months AradiaBot went from a small bot used by 10 servers to a bot used by thousands (over 19,000). Aradiabot currently features over 40 commands. (42 Currently) as well as many more to come.

Why should I choose this bot?

AradiaBot is fast, reliable and most importantly she just works. Theres no hassle or long set up screens, just a single command and AradiaBot will do the rest.
